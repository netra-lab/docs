# Utility

## Governance

$NETRA token will act as a governance token where holders can participate in the voting as well as creating proposal to decide the future of Netra protocol.

## Revenue Sharing

After [Phase 2](/concepts/netra-token/overview#economics-2), $NETRA token holders will be getting:

1. A percentage from every mint transaction of Netra NFT in Netra Platform
2. A percentage from every transaction of Netra NFT on secondary market sales in the marketplaces (OpenSea etc)

Rewards will be distributed and can be claimed based on the amount of $NETRA token users hold and staked in Netra.

## Supply

$NETRA token will have a fixed supply on TGE
